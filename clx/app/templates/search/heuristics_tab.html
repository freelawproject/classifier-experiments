Heuristics Overview

Heuristics are simple keyword rules that filter examples and are stored as tags. Two naming conventions for their associated tags are used:

  • h:qs:{querystring}:{label_slug} — querystring-based heuristic
  • h:fn:{function_name}:{label_slug} — custom function heuristic (registered in custom_heuristics.py)

Each heuristic can be flagged as a Minimal or Likely condition. We use these flags to guide sampling of training data.

Excluded () — example does not meet any Minimal condition.
Neutral () — neither explicitly Minimal-excluded nor Likely-matched.
Likely () — example meets any Likely condition; good candidates for positive sampling.
